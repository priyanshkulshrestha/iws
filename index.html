Introduction

Infinite Wellness Solutions Private Limited is a Private startup company incorporated on 16 March 2021. It is classified as a non-govt company and is registered at Registrar of Companies, Mumbai. Dr. Pranav Ingole and ID. Mayuri Gosewade are the founders of the company.
Infinite wellness solutions Pvt.Ltd is aplatform for promoting digital methods to create awareness among people regarding the usage of electronic techniques to pursue health care or avail the services of health sector of IWS. It mainly provides online health care products and services.
